The Missing Piece

IV drug users not captured by pharmaceutical companies despite being a target market for HIV and Hepatitis C portfolios.

Our system profiles, recruits, engages, and monetizes IV drug users through existing harm reduction innitiatives. We apply state of the art preventative healthcare interventions as a game-like series of incentives for IV drug users to take their meds. The most difficult pateints are now in good care, and everyone saves costs - win-win-win all around.

Today, pharma sends drug reps to prescribers, however, IV drug user patients do not see doctors - they are off the grid, hard to reach.
